|InTheGame_pros= 
* Thick frontal armor with few weakspots
* Outstanding DPM
* Massive gun arc for a turretless tank destroyer
* Great gun depression
* Good hitpoint pool for a tank destroyer

|InTheGame_cons= 
* Very low top speed, struggles to relocate
* Poor agility and hull traverse speed, easily outflanked
* Has a poorly armored lower plate, unlike the [[Tortoise]]
* Poor penetration and shell velocity
* Rear-mounted engine compartment is large and poorly armored
