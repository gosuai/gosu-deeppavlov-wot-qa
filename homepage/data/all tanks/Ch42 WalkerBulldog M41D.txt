|History= 
M41 modified by the Republic of China Army with a new Detroit Diesel 8V-71T diesel engine, enabling the tank to reach speeds of up to 72 km/h (44. Ch42 WalkerBulldog M41D 7 mph) and increasing its range to 450 km (279 miles). Ch42 WalkerBulldog M41D  The turret was altered to carry a Taiwanese-manufactured variant of the M32 known as the M32K1, as well as a co-axial Type 74 (FN MAG) general purpose machine gun. Ch42 WalkerBulldog M41D  The M32K1 has a fume extractor and a different muzzle brake, and has been upgraded with thermal imaging sights. Ch42 WalkerBulldog M41D
