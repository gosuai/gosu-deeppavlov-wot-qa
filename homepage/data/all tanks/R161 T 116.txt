|History= 
“In 1937, the head of the ABTU initiated the creation of a new reconnaissance tank. R161 T 116  New tactical and technical requirements were issued in the Autumn of 1937, it was stated the tank should not be amphibious and have the same wheel-track type of the BT series. R161 T 116  Work on the project was carried out by the design bureau of the Plant Nº 185. R161 T 116  It was planned to use an aircraft air-cooled engine and transmission of the PT-1 and T-29 tanks.
