|InTheGame_pros= 
* Hydropneumatic suspensions - can improve gun depression/elevation at low speed or when stationary 
* High alpha damage (joint highest for a Tier 10 medium tank) and good DPM
* Extremely sloped turret front and upper hull, can bounce many shots
* Very good acceleration, reverse speed and hull traverse speed
* Low profile, good camouflage values

|InTheGame_cons= 
* Armor relies entirely on sloping for protection, and is very thin
* Shell velocity and accuracy are subpar, can make sniping tricky
* Extremely vulnerable to HE damage, especially artillery
* Viewrange is rather poor for a Tier 10 medium tank
