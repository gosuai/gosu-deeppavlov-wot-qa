GB85 Cromwell Berlin  Generally, both styles are used in a single battle, starting as a support vehicle and switching to as aggressive flanker as opportunities present themselves for the tank to rush in with its amazing speed to chew enemies apart with its quick firing gun. GB85 Cromwell Berlin  However, it is a delicate tank and care must be taken to not take hits in the early stages of the game, as it will lower your ability to engage harder targets later on.
