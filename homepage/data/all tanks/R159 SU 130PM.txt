|InTheGame_pros= 

* Third best alpha damage of all Tier 8 tank destroyers, good penetration
* Good accuracy and aim time for a high caliber Russian gun
* High top speed and good acceleration, can relocate reasonably quickly
* Wide gun arc and decent gun depression
* Good camouflage values

|InTheGame_cons= <br />

* The gun cannot fully traverse 
* Low hitpoint pool and virtually unarmored, poor survivability
* Exceedingly vulnerable to ramming and HE damage
* Poor gun handling, can reliably hit only when stationary and fully aimed
* Limited gun elevation when aiming to the sides
