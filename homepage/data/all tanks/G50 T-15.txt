|History=
Designed by the company Škoda starting in 1940, it weighed between 11-13 tons and had a maximum speed of 50-60 km/h. G50 T-15   By 1942 the German government had lost interest in the development of the tank, but the company still continued development in-house. G50 T-15   Only four prototypes were made, and the T 15 never saw battle or entered active service. G50 T-15  
Note that it was supposed to be very modestly armored and armed like most light tanks, with a 50mm cannon KwK 39 L/60 gun and MG34 and 30mm armor at its weakest point. G50 T-15
