GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  It has a good mix of protection, damage output, and mobility, and can be used in multiple roles. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain

|InTheGame_pros= 
* Outstanding gun, arguably the best 120mm currently in the game 
* Extremely well-armored turret and well angled upper glacis
* Very good top speed and acceleration for a heavy tank
* Great gun depression, can use most hull down positions
* Excellent viewrange at 410 metres base

|InTheGame_cons= 
* Not counting the upper glacis, the hull is poorly armored
* While small, there is a thinly armored cupola weakspot on top of the turret
* Health pool is on the low side for a Tier 10 heavy tank
* Priority target for artillery and large caliber HE shells, especially when hulldown
* Worse DPM, accuracy, aim time and hull armor than techtree counterpart [[Super Conqueror]]

|InTheGame_performance=
To start off, the T95/FV4201 Cheiftain has an abundance of pros, with very good base stats, which can be improved further with equipment, crew skills and food/directives. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  In regards to its main cannon, it is an excellent one. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  It combines decent Rate of Fire (5. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 96), decent Alpha damage (440/440/530), good gun handling (0. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 13/0. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 13/0. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 11), decent dispersion (0. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 34) and high penetration overall (270/322/140). GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 

Getting to positions to leverage the gun is not very much of a problem for it, as it has good hp/t, ground resistances and top speed, especially for a heavy tank, as well as -10 gun depression, enough to reliably go hulldown. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 

Survivability-wise, the armor is not very good anywhere on the hull apart from the upper plate, which is surprisingly decent for a hull-down oriented tank. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  However, the turret is one of, if not the best turret in the game. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  It has a base 350mm thickness, meaning HE is hardly effective against it except for large caliber (150mm+), but the effective armor is high enough that even the [[Jagdpanzer E 100]] with its infamous 420mm penetration (the highest penetration in the game) HEAT shells cannot reliably penetrate it, as the one weakpoint on the Cheiftain's turret, the cupola, is rather small. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 

Like any heavy tank, its camouflage value isn't worth speaking of, but it does have an excellent 410 base meter view range, only behind the [[M48A5 Patton]], [[XM551 Sheridan]], [[Rheinmetall Panzerwagen]], [[121B]], and [[M60]] (420m) and tied or higher than everything else. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  It is also somewhat small for a Heavy tank, closer to that of the bigger mediums such as the [[M48A5 Patton]] or [[E 50 Ausf. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  M]]. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 

However, it does have some weaknesses. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  If forced into trading shots, it can suffer due to the amount of tanks with 490 or higher alpha, and combined with its relatively low Health (2,200) it can be very deadly. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  If the T95/FV4201 Cheiftain is forced to fight on flat ground, it will suffer due to its lacking reliable hull armor. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  Its biggest flaw,  though, may be its near overwhelming upsides and not enough downsides to make it balanced; in other words, it can be considered overpowered, or at least extremely strong. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain  As a result, it is a priority target, and attracts a lot of attention from artillery in particular. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain 

Overall, it is a very good vehicle in good hands, especially with proper equipment, crew skills and consumables/and or directives, but it is far from invulnerable. GB98 T95 FV4201 Chieftain
